ANANGOOR TEXTILE MILLS (P) LIMITED

Established in the year 1995 at Tirupur (Tamil nadu). We “Anangoor Textile Mills Private Limited" is a prominent manufacturer, exporter and supplier of world class quality of Open end yarn and Ring spun yarn to Domestic and international markets.

The management Team is supported by an experienced & technically qualified team.

Anangoor Textile Mills (P) Limited is formed by well qualified and experienced management team in the textile industry with a collective experience and has been instrumental for the sustained growth of the company.

WE ARE

Anangoor Textile Mills (P) Limited Unit I

Anangoor Textile Mills (P) Limited Unit I established in the year 1996 has a capacity of 5000 Rotors. The mill has a complete range of state of the art open end machinery including the latest generation from Trutzschler Germany,Rieter Switzerland.

Mill produces 1400 tons of kora yarn and 3100 tons of OE mélange and OE colour yarn per annum.

Anangoor Textile Mills (P) Limited Unit II

Anangoor Textile Mills (P) Limited Unit II established in the year 2007 has a capacity of 21600 spindles has a complete range of state of the art machineries from LMW ltd, Coimbatore and autoconer from Muratec – Japan.

Mill produces around 5000 tons of combed and carded cotton yarn per annum.
